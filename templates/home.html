Library perspectives:

Indian Institute of Information Technology Pune (IIITPune) Library started with the institution in 2016. It has developed with academic collection with huge number of titles and volumes, journals and magazines both in printed version and electronic format. Presently Library has more than 3200 books with 403 titles in two branches of engineering i.e. Electronics & Communication Engineering and , Computer Science Engineering, , at UG, level.

Library has institutional membership of two renowned online databases, i.e. World e-book library and South Asian Achieve. These online databases make the library competent to learning enthusiasm, higher studies and research of students and faculty members.

The library is spacious and divided into different sections to facilitate proper utilization of available resources. Library has been automated with I SLIM software and providing good number of services like book search, reservation, renewal and account checking details through Web OPAC. All the available books and magazines are classified by DDC – 23nd edition, and enabled bar-coded circulation services. The library is taking constant efforts to provide relevant resources to its users by using different technologies.
